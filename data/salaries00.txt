[These are the figures listed on the cap for this season.  Players that 
are cut with guaranteed contracts still count against the cap.  For 
contracts that were bought out by a team, the buyout amount counts 
against the cap.  

For veteran players with more than 4 years of experience signed to minimum
contracts, their salary cap number is only $510,000, even though they 
actually earn more.  Per the CBA, the league pays part of those salaries.

For Shawn Bradley, he is actually earning about $2 million instead of the 
$7,560,000 that counts against the cap due to failing to reach certain 
statistical numbers in the past (this is a special case and I'm not aware 
of any others).

The scale salary for 1st round draft picks that were not signed and not 
renounced (ie, Frederic Weis and New York) still count against the cap 
even though the player does not receive any money.  

Suspended players still count against the cap even though they are not 
receiving any money (both Derek Harper and Bison Dele announced their 
retirement, but Detroit chose to place them on the suspended list).  

There are a number of players on the list that seem to be unrenounced 
free agents that still count against the cap even though they are not
receiving any money.  These are players who completed their last 
contract with the team and have not signed another contract and their
last team has not renounced them.  I've marked these players with a 
note of "[completed contract*]".

If a player has been released and has a salary of less than $301,875,
then that was the amount that the player earned before he was released.
The player is no longer receiving any money from the team.

The list does not include players signed signed February (ie, Dennis 
Rodman) - those players earn a pro-rated minimum salary (ie, Dennis 
Rodman is earning $453,941).  I've added them to the end of the team's 
salary list, but have not listed their salary (besides "minimum") or 
changed the team's total salary.
   
I've marked the above instances where I've noticed them.]
